The Best Movies (According to Matthew)

My Top 3 Favourite Movies

Indiana Jones and the Last Crusade

This was the actually the first Indiana Jones movie I saw as a kid (I watched them out of order) but holds out as my favourite. By the time the third Indiana Jones hit the screen, Spielberg and Lucas knew what worked. It also ties the franchise up neatly (I don't recognize and Indiana Jones after this).

JoJo Rabbit

Kind of weird that I have two movies with Nazis in a row, but JoJo Rabbit was brilliant and beautiful. I had never heard of it prior to watching so it was a fun suprise.

The Lego Movies

I'm a big animation fan and Lego was a staple of my childhood. When I first watched the Lego movie in theatres I was floored with the amount of care and detail they put into it.
